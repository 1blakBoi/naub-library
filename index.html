FAQs

NAUB is a public university in Biu, Borno State, Nigeria, established to advance national security and technological innovation, focusing on both military and civilian education.

No, NAUB is open to both military personnel and civilians, designed to provide quality education to a diverse student body

Prospective students must have a minimum of five O'Level credits, including Mathematics and English, and must score at least 180 in the Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME). The admission policy consists of a mix of military and civilian applicants, as well as specific subject requirements based on the chosen program

NAUB offers programs through several faculties including Science, Technology, Military Technology, Social Sciences, and Environmental Studies, among others. The university focuses on producing graduates with essential skills relevant to contemporary challenges
